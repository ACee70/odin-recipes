Syrup Recipe

Description

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • White sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and water in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Bring to a boil, stirring, until sugar has dissolved.
  3. Pour into a clean jar and seal with a tight-fitting lid;
  4. Store in the refrigerator for up to one month.
